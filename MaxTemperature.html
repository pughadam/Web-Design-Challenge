Max Temperature

When looking at the data and graphs, there appears to be a very strong correlation between Latitude and Max Temperature.

The closer you get to the equator it appears the tighter the range of temperatures. When looking at the graphs, it appears that outliers Max Temperatures start to show themselves around 20 degrees from Zero. The data shows that the further away you are from the equator the cooler the Max Temperature will be. Closer studying of this data is needed with global warming coming so quickly.
